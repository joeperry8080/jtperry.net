Who am I?

I am a tech enthusiast community college professor preparing students for careers in IT. I have nearly 20 years of professional experience, both in corporate IT and as an educator. I hold multiple certifications from CompTIA and am always keeping my skills updated.

What is this website?

I decided to build this site as a personal project for one of the courses I teach, and am dedicating it to my students. The resoruces I plan to add to this site are from common questions that I receive from students every term. Think of it as a way to catalog the information so that it is easy to find.

This site is a work in progress, I will be adding more resoruces and information on an ongoing basis.
